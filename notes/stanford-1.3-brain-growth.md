### Short Notes: Evidence That the Brain Can Grow and Change

#### London Taxi Drivers

* Taxi drivers must learn **20,000 streets** and **25,000 landmarks**.
* After training, their **hippocampus** (memory and navigation area) becomes larger.
* When they retire, it shrinks back.
* Shows that the brain changes based on use.

#### Juggling Study

* People who practiced juggling improved their skills.
* Their brain areas for **vision and movement** grew stronger.
* Practice caused physical changes in the brain.

#### 7-Year-Old Girl's Recovery

* Half of her brain was removed to stop severe seizures.
* Doctors expected long-term disability.
* She recovered movement abilities because her brain formed new connections.
* Shows the brain can adapt and reorganize.

#### Brain Plasticity

* The brain is very **plastic** (able to change and adapt).
* Even a **6-week training program** can change brain structure.
* Small amounts of daily practice can rewire the brain.

#### Key Message

* The brain grows and changes through learning and practice.
* Regular effort in math can strengthen the brain and improve ability over time.
