# **Omo Osama's Story – Life, Loss, and Hope in Gaza**

* **Omo Osama** is a **37-year-old mother** living with her family in Gaza.
* Her family has spent their lives surrounded by walls and the sea, with very limited freedom of movement.
* Before the conflict, she lived a simple life and worked in a **kindergarten**.
* Her husband previously transported bricks but now works as a **taxi driver**.

### Life During Conflict

* Their neighbourhood was heavily damaged during the war.
* Many families lost their homes, rebuilt them, and then saw them destroyed again in later conflicts.
* Omo Osama tries to provide a normal childhood for her children despite the uncertainty and fear around them.
* She worries because no one knows when violence may return.

### Tragic Loss

* On **17 July**, her family left their home because of bombing and took shelter in a school.
* On **30 July**, the school was hit by shelling.
* During the attack, Omo Osama lost **two of her children**, including her son **Usama**.
* She speaks directly to her children, saying they will always remain in her heart and prayers.
* She wishes no mother would ever have to experience such a loss.

### Impact on the Family

* The deaths deeply affected the whole family.
* Her middle son, **Musa**, changed after the tragedy:

  * He no longer wants to attend school.
  * He cries frequently.
  * He wakes up at night screaming from fear and trauma.
* Omo Osama also struggles with fear and grief while trying to stay strong for her family.

### Seeking Help and Recovery

* The emotional pain became overwhelming, so she searched for support.
* She discovered many other people facing similar struggles.
* She believes children can heal only when parents are emotionally strong.
* She stresses the importance of:

  * Strong family support
  * Education
  * Good values and beliefs
  * Love and tenderness

### Hopes for the Future

* Omo Osama wants a **safe and dignified life** for her husband and children.
* She hopes the cycle of violence will end, although she knows it may be beyond her control.
* She wishes for peace so families can live normal lives again.
* Despite everything, she remains patient and determined to continue living.

### A New Beginning

* Omo Osama learns that she is **pregnant**.
* The pregnancy gives her renewed hope for the future.
* She prays for strength, peace, and the ability to keep moving forward despite her losses.

### Main Themes

* **Effects of war on civilians**
* **Loss and grief**
* **Trauma and mental health**
* **Strength of mothers and families**
* **Hope and resilience**
* **Children's experiences during conflict**
* **Desire for peace and security**
