### Short Notes: Praise and Mindset

#### Importance of a Growth Mindset

* A growth mindset is important for learning any subject, especially math.
* Many students have a fixed mindset about math because of common misconceptions.

#### The Problem with Being Called "Smart"

* Praising someone for being "smart" can create a fixed mindset.
* It suggests intelligence is something fixed rather than something that can grow.
* When students later fail, they may start doubting their abilities.

#### Better Type of Praise

* Praising **effort, hard work, and persistence** encourages a growth mindset.
* It teaches that success comes from practice and learning.

#### Research Study

* Students were given the same math problem.
* One group was praised for being **smart**.
* Another group was praised for **working hard**.

#### Results

* Students praised for being smart usually chose the **easy problem**.
* Students praised for working hard usually chose the **hard problem**.
* The hard-working group wanted to challenge themselves and learn more.

#### Key Message

* Focus on effort and improvement rather than being "smart."
* Challenges help people learn and grow.
* Mistakes and difficult problems are opportunities for learning.
