### Short Notes

#### Activities That Help Math Learning

* Building with shapes, origami, and LEGO helps develop math skills.
* Board games and puzzles can improve mathematical thinking.
* Some apps and computer games also support math learning.

#### Stereotypes and Learning

* Construction and building toys are often marketed more to boys than girls.
* This is an example of **stereotyped thinking**.
* Stereotypes can limit opportunities for some people.

#### Important Messages

* Do not let stereotypes decide what you can learn or enjoy.
* Ideas, learning, and creativity are for everyone.
* Anyone can succeed in math regardless of gender or background.
* Never believe someone who says a subject is "not for you."

#### Key Takeaway

* Learning opportunities should be available to everyone.
* Building, creating, solving puzzles, and exploring ideas help develop important thinking skills.
