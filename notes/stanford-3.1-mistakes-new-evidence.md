### Short Notes: Mistakes Help the Brain Grow

#### Mistakes and Brain Growth

* Making mistakes causes the brain to grow and form stronger connections.
* Brain growth happens when you **struggle and think** about mistakes.
* Simply getting the correct answer does not create as much brain growth.

#### Importance of Challenging Problems

* Difficult problems help the brain develop more than easy ones.
* Struggling with math is a normal and valuable part of learning.
* Mistakes should be seen as opportunities to improve.

#### Example: Thomas Edison

* His teachers believed he was not capable of learning.
* He made about **1,000 unsuccessful attempts** before inventing the light bulb.
* He viewed each mistake as a step toward success, not a failure.

#### Key Message

* Mistakes are an important part of learning.
* Persistence and effort lead to success.
* Hard work and perseverance are more important than natural talent.
