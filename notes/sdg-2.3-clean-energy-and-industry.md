## Clean Energy and Industry (SDG 2.3)

The **third of the six transformations**: shift from a fossil-fuel economy to **clean, safe energy** + **ecologically sound industry** (the circular economy). Central to **SDG 13** (climate), **SDG 7** (modern energy for all), and **SDG 12** (sustainable production).

**The scale of the problem:** ~**85% of primary energy** still comes from fossil fuels. That releases roughly **40 billion tonnes of CO₂** per year — mainly from energy and industrial processes.

---

### The 3-Pillar Approach to Decarbonising Energy

**Pillar 1 — Zero-carbon electricity**
* Electricity will be the dominant energy carrier of the 21st century: powers the digital world, vehicles, and heating (heat pumps replacing furnaces).
* Zero-carbon sources: **wind, solar, hydroelectric, geothermal, biofuels, ocean power, nuclear**.
* Each country picks based on its geography: sunny deserts → solar; windy coastlines → wind; mountains → hydro.
* Challenge: **intermittency** (solar only when sunny, wind only when blowing).
  * Solutions: **long-distance transmission grids** (balancing across regions) + **storage** (pumped hydro = use surplus electricity to pump water uphill, release through turbines later).

**Pillar 2 — Electrification of energy users**
* Replace fossil fuels *at the point of use*:
  * **Cars** → battery EVs or hydrogen fuel cells.
  * **Heavy shipping/aviation** → hydrogen combustion or synthetic liquid hydrocarbons (captured from the air using renewable energy — not dug up).
* Technologies largely exist already.

**Pillar 3 — Energy efficiency**
* Less energy needed = easier transition.
* How: **smart appliances**, better **building insulation** (reduce heating/cooling demand).

---

### The Circular Economy (SDG 12)

Industrial processes must **clean up after themselves** — "closing the loop":
* **Green chemistry**: redesign plastics so they can be recycled; use biodegradable materials.
* **Waste as input**: use industrial waste as raw material for the next process rather than dumping in landfills (landfills emit methane, a potent greenhouse gas).
* **Recycling + energy recovery**: waste → energy or direct recycling, not landfill.

---

### Key Message

Decarbonising the energy system = **zero-carbon electricity** + **electrification** + **energy efficiency**. Combine that with the **circular economy** to clean up industrial waste, and you get a sustainable development path that protects the climate and human health simultaneously.
