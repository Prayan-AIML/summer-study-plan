### Short Notes: Positive Messages and Learning

#### Challenges Help You Grow

* Hard problems are better for learning than easy ones.
* Struggling with difficult tasks helps the brain grow stronger.
* Avoiding challenges can limit learning and improvement.

#### Power of Messages

* Messages from others can quickly affect how people think about themselves.
* Negative messages can lower confidence and achievement.
* Positive messages encourage effort and success.

#### Research Findings

* Children performed worse when parents said they were not good at math.
* Students performed better when teachers showed belief in their abilities.
* Feeling supported and believed in can improve achievement.

#### Importance of Self-Belief

* The messages you tell yourself are very important.
* Believe that you can learn, improve, and succeed.
* Do not let negative comments define your abilities.

#### Key Message

* Choose challenges instead of easy tasks.
* Focus on effort, growth, and learning.
* Positive beliefs and encouragement can have a powerful effect on success.
