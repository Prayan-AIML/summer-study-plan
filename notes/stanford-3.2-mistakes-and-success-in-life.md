### Short Notes: The Importance of Mistakes and Taking Risks

#### Mistakes Lead to Success

* Successful people often make **more mistakes** because they take more risks.
* Mistakes are a natural part of learning and improvement.
* Taking on difficult challenges helps people grow.

#### Example: Michael Jordan

* One of the greatest basketball players in history.
* Missed over **9,000 shots** and lost many games.
* Believed that repeated failures were the reason for his success.

#### Habits of Successful People

* Try new and unusual ideas.
* Are comfortable making mistakes.
* Stay open to new experiences.
* Explore ideas without judging them too quickly.
* Keep going even when things are difficult.
* Are willing to challenge traditional ways of thinking.

#### Key Message

* Do not fear mistakes or failure.
* Take risks, accept challenges, and learn from every experience.
* Success comes from persistence, creativity, and learning from mistakes.
